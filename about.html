AXEL

A story about Guilt

The Story

A psychological, narrative, choose your own, horror game about guilt. Explore YOUR old school and try to remember YOUR past. The good and the bad. But especially all the bad YOU did.

Explore YOUR abandoned school, try to remember, and piece together why you feel this immeasurable amount of Guilt walking these halls.

Gameplay

Choose from varied choice boxes on where to move next. Experience an ending fit for your choices.

  • Collection system
  • Inventory management
  • Pixel hunting challenge
  • Typing puzzle
  • 5 different endings, but only 1 for YOU

Themes

This game explores heavy themes including guilt, bullying, loss and suicide.

Player discretion is advised. The narrative deals with mature content and psychological horror elements.
